🌟 Funke Akindele – Biography

Full Name: Akindele Olufunke Ayotunde

Date of Birth: August 24, 1977

Place of Birth: Ikorodu, Lagos State, Nigeria

Profession: Actress, Filmmaker, Producer, Politician

Years Active: 1998 – present

🎬 Early Career

Funke Akindele began her acting career in the 1990s, rising to fame with the sitcom "I Need to Know" (1998–2002). Her breakout role came in 2008 with the release of Jenifa, a comedy film that became a massive hit and led to the award-winning series Jenifa’s Diary.

🏆 Achievements

  • Winner of the Africa Movie Academy Award (AMAA) for Best Actress in a Leading Role (2009)
  • Creator and star of the hit TV series Jenifa’s Diary
  • Named among the 100 most influential Africans by New African magazine
  • Directed Omo Ghetto: The Saga, Nollywood’s highest-grossing film

🌍 Personal Life & Influence

Funke holds a Law degree from the University of Lagos and a diploma in Mass Communication. In 2022, she entered politics and ran as a Deputy Governor candidate in Lagos under the PDP. Her work uplifts social topics affecting women, youth, and behavior, making her one of the most impactful voices in Nigerian entertainment.

📺 Notable Works

  • Jenifa (2008)
  • Jenifa’s Diary (TV Series)
  • Omo Ghetto: The Saga (2020)
  • Battle on Buka Street (2022)
  • A Tribe Called Judah (2023)
