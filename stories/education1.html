📚 5 Study Hacks for Success

Date: June 28, 2025

Subtitle: Simple tricks students can use to boost grades easily.

1. Use the Pomodoro Technique ⏱️

Study in focused 25-minute sessions with 5-minute breaks in between. After 4 sessions, take a longer break (15–30 minutes). This helps improve concentration and reduces burnout.

2. Teach What You Learn 👩🏾‍🏫

Explaining what you've studied to someone else forces you to understand the material better. If you can teach it, you truly know it!

3. Study Before Bed 🛏️

Reviewing notes before sleeping helps your brain retain information better during rest. Just don’t cram—review calmly.

4. Create Visual Summaries 🧠

Turn your notes into mind maps, diagrams, or flashcards. Visual learning helps your brain process complex information faster and more effectively.

5. Use Active Recall, Not Just Reading 🔁

Instead of just re-reading, quiz yourself regularly. Close your book and try to recall what you just studied. It's one of the most powerful ways to strengthen memory.

Bonus Tip 🎯

Avoid multitasking. Focus on one subject at a time in a distraction-free environment. Music is fine, but turn off notifications!
