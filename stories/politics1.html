The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) has announced plans to introduce a fully digital voting system in Nigeria ahead of the 2027 general elections. The commission says this is a major step toward ensuring transparency, efficiency, and increased voter participation.

According to INEC Chairman Prof. Mahmood Yakubu, the new system will allow voters to cast their ballots electronically, starting with pilot programs in selected urban and rural regions by late 2026.
