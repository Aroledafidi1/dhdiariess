🎓 Top Local & International Scholarships for Nigerian Students (2025)

Last Updated: June 28, 2025

🇳🇬 Local Scholarships

1. MTN Foundation Scholarship

  • For: 2nd and 3rd year students in public universities/polytechnics
  • Amount: ₦200,000/year
  • CGPA requirement: 3.5 or equivalent
  • Apply here

2. Agbami Medical & Engineering Scholarship

  • For: Medical, Engineering & Health Science students
  • Amount: ₦100,000/year
  • Funded by: Chevron & Star Deepwater
  • Apply here

3. NNPC/TotalEnergies Scholarship

  • For: 100-level undergraduates in any discipline
  • Amount: ₦150,000/year
  • Apply here

4. Jim Ovia Foundation Scholarship

  • For: All Nigerian undergraduates
  • Full tuition and maintenance support
  • Apply here

5. Lagos State Bursary & Scholarship

  • For: Lagos State indigenes
  • Amount varies
  • Visit LASG portal
