🎤 Burna Boy Makes History at UEFA Final

Date: June 1, 2025

Afrobeats superstar Burna Boy continues to break boundaries, becoming the first African artist to headline the UEFA Champions League Final Kick-Off Show, which took place in Munich, Germany.

Burna Boy lit up the stage with hits like "Last Last" and "City Boys", thrilling millions of viewers worldwide. The show was broadcast to over 200 countries, giving Nigerian and African music even more global recognition.

This performance marks yet another milestone in Burna Boy's career, after headlining major concerts at Madison Square Garden, London Stadium, and Coachella.

"This is more than music, it’s about making Africa proud," the Grammy-winning artist said during a post-performance interview. Fans online have described the show as “electrifying,” “iconic,” and “a win for the entire continent.”
