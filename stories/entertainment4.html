🎶 Tems Unveils Debut Album: *Born in the Wild*

Date: June 7, 2025

Grammy-winning Nigerian singer Tems is officially stepping into a new chapter with the release of her highly anticipated debut album, *Born in the Wild*. After dominating global charts with collaborations like “Essence” with Wizkid and “Wait for U” with Future and Drake, Tems is now ready to fully showcase her sound.

The 15-track album includes hit singles like “Love Me Jeje,” “Born in the Wild,” and “Wickedest.” Tems described the album as “a journey of personal growth, freedom, and creative healing.”

Early reviews praise the album’s unique blend of R&B, soul, and Afrobeats. Rolling Stone calls it “a stunning debut that blends emotional depth with sonic elegance.” Fans are already buzzing online, calling it “album of the year.”

📀 Track Highlights

  • Love Me Jeje – A smooth rework of the 1997 Nigerian classic
  • Turn Me Up – High-energy ballad with reggae influences
  • Unbothered – A confidence anthem already trending on TikTok

Tems is also expected to go on a world tour starting in July 2025, with shows planned in Lagos, London, Paris, and New York.

🌍 A Global Star from Nigeria

Tems has consistently raised the bar for women in African music. From her soulful voice to her genre-bending style, *Born in the Wild* marks not just a debut, but a bold statement that she’s here to stay.
